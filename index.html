About Jeffrey

Built from the Inside Out

Jeff Roth knows what it takes to lead a high school athletic program — not from a distance, but from experience. As a former Division I athlete, high school coach, and current athletic director with over 20 years in the field, Jeff has lived every stage of the journey he now helps others navigate.

His coaching practice is rooted in the belief that great athletic administrators and coaches are made, not born. With the right mentorship, intentional development, and a structured plan for growth, emerging leaders can accelerate their trajectory and build high performing programs that last.

Jeff founded Roth Performance Coaching to give athletic directors and coaches the resource he wished he had — a trusted partner who understands the unique pressures, politics, and passion of high school athletics.
